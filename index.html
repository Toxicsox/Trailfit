About

Built for Sunday hiking adventures — one place to find moderate-to-challenging trails within an hour of Browns Bay. No more cross-referencing websites every Saturday night.

Sunday suggestion

Picks a trail each week based on the Sunday weather forecast, how recently you've done each trail, and your fitness progression. Avoids repeating trails done in the last 4 weeks.

Fitness progression

Three levels: Explorer → Trekker → Summit Seeker. As you log more hikes, challenging trails unlock. The app tracks your progress and highlights what you're ready for next.

Weather

Sunday forecasts from Open-Meteo — free, no API key needed. Shows conditions for Waitākere, Tāwharanui/North, North Shore, and Hunua zones.

Track conditions

Always check before heading out:

  • Auckland Council — track closures
  • DOC Auckland parks
  • Kauri Dieback closures
